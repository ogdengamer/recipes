Oriental Chicken Salad

Source: Lemon Poppy Café

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts, cooked and sliced or shredded
  • 8 oz dry bow-tie pasta (or similar), cooked and cooled
  • 1 package broccoli slaw
  • 1 cup mandarin orange segments, drained
  • 1 cup cashews
  • 1/2 cup sunflower seeds
  • Dressing:
    • 1/3 cup oil
    • 1/3 cup rice vinegar
    • 1/3 cup sugar
    • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
    • 1 teaspoon salt
    • 1/2 teaspoon pepper

Directions

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions. Drain, rinse under cold water, and set aside.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cooked chicken, pasta, and broccoli slaw.
  3. Add the mandarin oranges, cashews, and sunflower seeds.
  4. In a separate bowl or jar, whisk together the oil, rice vinegar, sugar, soy sauce, salt, and pepper until the sugar is dissolved.
  5. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  6. Chill before serving, if desired.
