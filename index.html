Crockpot Chicken Noodle Soup

Source: Slow Cooker Café

Ingredients

  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 8 cups chicken broth
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 8 oz egg noodles (or other small pasta)

Directions

  1. Add onion, celery, carrots, chicken, broth, bay leaf, garlic salt, and pepper to a slow cooker.
  2. Cover and cook on LOW for 6–8 hours, until the chicken is very tender.
  3. Remove chicken, shred with two forks, and return to the slow cooker.
  4. Stir in the noodles and cook on HIGH for about 30 minutes, or until the noodles are tender.
  5. Remove bay leaf and serve hot.
