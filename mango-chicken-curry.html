Mango Chicken Curry

Source: Simply Recipes

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped (about 1 1/2 to 2 cups)
  • 2 red bell peppers, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, minced
  • 3/4 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 3/4 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 2 mangos, peeled and sliced
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper
  • 1 cup low-fat coconut milk (or 1/2 cup coconut milk plus 2 tablespoons heavy cream)
  • 1 1/3 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs or breasts, cut in 1-inch pieces
  • 1/3 cup golden raisins (optional)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Directions

  1. Heat the oil in a large saut√© pan over medium heat. Add the onion and bell peppers and cook until softened.
  2. Stir in the garlic, ginger, cumin, and coriander; cook for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Add the mangos, brown sugar, tomato paste, and crushed red pepper; cook for 2 minutes, stirring.
  4. Pour in the coconut milk and bring the mixture to a gentle simmer.
  5. Add the chicken pieces and raisins (if using). Cover and simmer for about 20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and tender.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust sweetness or acidity as desired and serve hot with rice.
